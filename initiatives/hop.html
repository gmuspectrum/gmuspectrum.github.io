Some Person

"My family's Portuguese, everyone speaks Portuguese. I moved here when I was eight; I had to learn English in elementary school. But to learn English, I had to learn Spanish first...my family was really the key component to help me get acclimated here and it carries through to my academic journey because they've always been in my corner...the highest degree that my family had gotten was some trade school...I've never felt pressure to do grad school, to do anything else. I'm here because I want to be. And I'm the oldest grandson too. So I'm the first gen, and the one who is going through all the paths and showing my cousins that they and my sister, that they can do it; whatever they want."
